Welcome

Welcome to Faith Based Pilot — where faith, aviation, and purpose intersect. We're here to lift others by giving guidance to truth, and our own purpose is everything to do with it.

Mission

Our mission is to encourage others into discovering their God-given calling while building up the body of Christ. We point toward identity in Christ and away from worldly confusion.

Why Anonymity

We believe the message matters more than the messenger. This platform's content is shared for edification, not personal gain or fame. To know who we are, look to the words we’ve written and the life we lead beyond the screen.
