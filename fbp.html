Faith Based Pilot (.net)

Watch. Listen. Strengthen your faith.

FaithBasedPilot.net is the official watching and listening hub for Faith Based Pilot — a clean, focused place to experience messages about faith, fitness, and flight without distractions or feed hunting. If you want a direct way to watch and listen to FBP content, you’re in the right place.

Mission

Faith Based Pilot exists to encourage strength, discipline, and truth in everyday life — in the gym, in the cockpit, at work, and at home. Each message aims to challenge the mind, strengthen conviction, and point to a life lived boldly in faith.

Why This .NET Site Exists

.net and .org serve different purposes. The .net site is intentionally built for media — a home to watch and listen. The FaithBasedPilot.org site focuses on written studies, articles, and resources. Keeping them separate makes both clearer and easier to use.

Quick guide:
  • .net → watching & listening hub (videos, audio, series)
  • .org → reading & research (long-form writing, references, resources)

YouTube & Rumble

Faith Based Pilot publishes on both YouTube and Rumble. YouTube is the primary platform for reach and accessibility. Rumble operates as a secure backup in case YouTube ever restricts or removes content. This dual presence protects the message and keeps it available.
